When the parade was cancelled I felt a little bad, but what could they do?
I've never been to a pride on purpose (last year I was on a studying holiday in Dublin and when we went in Belfast for a trip we saw thw pride - even if nobody knew there would have been it). These photos you see are the ones I took in Belfast.
By the way, I didn't really join the parade, I just saw it, but there I decided I would have joined the Milan pride in 2020.
I was excited to see the Virtual Global Pride, but I didn't like it: I saw just about an hour of the pride, but it seemed to be badly organized to me, too divided. Every person who made a speech spoke for about a minute and then appeared another person who told other different things. And the songs: they were put almost casually in the video...
I don't know, in the Official website it was described and i expected something way better.
Now I hope I'll be at the Milan pride next year :) .
